click on add new
> it will redirect you to page which will ask you to select category.

> upon selected category will populate its id which will be captured as a snapshot router event.
> having captured category id, the a form will just be rendered with a 1 or 2 form fields which will accept items details and on submit event, it will submit the item details/data concatenated with an id of category which will appear as a foreign key in items collections.
